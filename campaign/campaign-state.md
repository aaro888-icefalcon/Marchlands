## Story so far
**Scene 1 — the Crown's intake at the Reach.** Shake came to bound at the front of the intake line, lumped in with captured "Stonebay brigands." Built diegetically as a NYC vascular surgeon / Battle Smith Artificer who crossed with his mind and class but no gear. Revealed himself a healer — which made him wanted by all sides. Asked for tools to save a dying captive (**failed Persuasion ×2**), refused the rigged test and blamed Godric (**failed Persuasion**) — the man died, Hask won the yard, and Godric, boxed, handed Shake to the **Mossdrowen wardens** to conscript. *That faction-claim triggered the catastrophe:* the **Moor** came down, shattered the wall, and the Reach became a killing-ground. Shake **scavenged the kit-cart** (Stealth 21), got his magic back, and with **Sefa lifted the pen's gate-bar** (double nat-20 + Guidance) to free ~100 trapped drift-come. A **Silent Image** failed to deter the Moor (it fed on the crowd's rear). Shake **broke free clean** east with Sefa. Godric's fate unknown; Shake's public magic was witnessed.

**Scene 2 — the barrow-heath.** The road climbed into a field of howes where ~20 Reach survivors sheltered against a great pilgrimage-mound. A heath-woman named the wrongness — the Working has cracked the barrow-seals; the **barrow-cold** meant one had risen. Shake's evacuation **failed**; panic broke the column, a **barrow-wight** killed the slowest, the crowd scattered. Shake grabbed the **nurse**, sent **Sefa** with the **child**, and ran (**nat-20**) clear of the howe-field; the wight, bound to its dead-ground, would not follow. **Two saved of twenty-odd.** CF → 7.

**Scene 3 — Stanwic.** Arrived at dawn to a lynch-mob (Bron) set to feed six drift-come to the heath. Shake had **Sefa** make the pragmatic case (Guidance + backing; **Persuasion 21**): turned the mob, saved the six and his band — into a servitude bargain. **In** Stanwic, on a leash; Bron a new enemy; a promise to help hold the wall against the dead. CF → 6.
