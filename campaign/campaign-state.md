the line with a wax tablet. Over the pen, **four powers** argue what the strangers are *for* (Godric/oath ·

(Caine's ordeal · Maerwyn's reading). The test is about to begin — and the moor-troll is already coming
down out of the north on the smell of the largest helpless crowd in the realm. **Loaded gun:** the troll
lands mid-scene (the Turn / catastrophe), the Reach becomes a besieged killing-ground, and the way out is
the five roads.
