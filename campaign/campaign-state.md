## The premise (revised by the player)
- **The Crossing:** ~8 million New Yorkers torn into Hethrun in one black second, scattered in clusters. A cluster landed at the Reach.
- **Intake is by the Crown.** The strangers ("drift-come") are detained and processed by the Crown at the Reach.
- **Three powers pull on the strangers:**
  - **The Crown (Reeve Godric):** wants the headache *gone* — indifferent to any single stranger's fate — but will **not** let **Stonebay** take them, nor wear a massacre if **Edmund** gets his way on Crown ground.
  - **Count Edmund:** wants the strangers **executed** (a danger/burden best ended); pressing the Crown for the authority.
  - **Stonebay (Ranulf):** wants to **acquire** them for the eastern markets; agents work the intake.
- **No religious faction / no demon-test** (removed). **No mystic apex / monster-siege loaded** — drama is political.
- **PC:** a New Yorker pulled over by the Crossing, **English-speaking**, built **diegetically at intake**. (Old PC "Shake Rao" + all prior story scratched.)
