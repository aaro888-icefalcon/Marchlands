## Story so far
**Scene 1 — the Crown's intake at the Reach.** Shake came to bound at the front of the intake line, lumped in with captured "Stonebay brigands." Built diegetically as a NYC vascular surgeon / Battle Smith Artificer who crossed with his mind and class but no gear. Revealed himself a healer — which made him wanted by all sides. Asked for tools to save a dying captive (**failed Persuasion ×2**), refused the rigged test and blamed Godric (**failed Persuasion**) — the man died, Hask won the yard, and Godric, boxed (won't hang for Edmund, won't sell to Stonebay), handed Shake to the **Mossdrowen wardens** to conscript. *That faction-claim triggered the catastrophe:* the **Moor** came down, shattered the west wall, and the Reach became a killing-ground. In the chaos Shake **scavenged the confiscated-kit cart clean** (Stealth 21), got his magic back, and — with **Sefa** — **lifted the pen's gate-bar** (Athletics, double nat-20 + Guidance) to free ~100 trapped drift-come, ringing the dinner bell. A **Silent Image** wall failed to deter the Moor (it fed on the crowd's rear, which bought the front the gate). Shake **broke free clean** east with Sefa. Godric's fate unknown; people died; Shake's public magic was witnessed.
