# Seed Deck — The Crossing at Skellan's Reach
_Built at setup from the live ledger + the opening's canon + honest generator rolls. **35 cards.**_
_Refresh to 30–40 each bookkeeping (`world-model.md` §5). A seed is **consumed** when a scene / Turning
Point / Random Event invokes it — replace it next refresh._

## Channel 1 — the live world ledger (clocks · threats · powers' next moves) — ~half the deck
1. **Moor's Hunger arrives:** out past the wall the screaming dogs go silent all at once — then the gate-timber groans. The troll is here. *(loaded gun · Moor's Hunger 0/8)*
2. **The ordeal reaches Shake's row:** Caine's acolytes lift the consecrated iron out of the brazier, glowing. *(Codex test)*
3. **Maerwyn cuts in:** she forces to the front and demands the *reading* before the ordeal — buying the strangers (and Shake) a minute. *(Old Faith test)*
4. **Godric makes the oath-offer** to the strongest-looking strangers: fed, shielded, the Crown's — today. *(oath)*
5. **The Tally-man's contract:** a parchment pressed into a desperate hand in the rope's shadow; ink, a thumbprint, done. *(coercive contract)*
6. **Hask's coffle moves:** the chain rattles down the line as the factor marks the "fit ones" for the dig. *(enslavement)*
7. **Brona's quiet word:** the wood takes anyone who'll hold a spear — out of the pen, clear of the court. *(enlistment)*
8. **The Red Captain waits:** he tests the chains, and the guards' rhythm, for the half-second he needs.
9. **A New Yorker breaks** — bolts the rail — and the guards make an example of him. *(the mob turns)*
10. **The purge widens:** word arrives that another town simply burned its strangers. *(Codex purge 0/8)*
11. **Slavers at the wall:** buyers on the road outside, taking "strays" off the fyrd cheap. *(Slavers 0/6)*
12. **The south is impatient:** a rider — the Witan wants Cuthbert's Coronation answer. *(Coronation 0/12)*
13. **The undercroft groans:** the Working stirred whatever's been sealed five centuries below the road-station.
14. **The kit-cart in the bailey:** half-guarded — Shake's longsword, shield, and tools are sitting on it. *(§8 escape lever)*
15. **Sefa flinches:** the young huscarl can't stomach the brazier; she looks away at the wrong moment.
16. **Powers nearly come to blows** over a single stranger — the pen's order frays.
17. **The faiths' deadlock** stalls every claim: nobody is taken until the test is settled.
18. **A word on Akhil:** a stranger swears they saw "an Indian medic, young" in another cluster, days back. *(personal thread)*

## Channel 2 — canon near the PC (Brackenford · the Reach · the five roads)
19. **Eorl Cuthbert's wildcard vote:** everyone courts it; his reeve holds the Reach for the Crown.
20. **The Aureothan road-station:** five roads to five eorldoms over one sealed undercroft.
21. **The boy-king Hadric & the Aurean debt:** a Regency that needs bodies and coin, now.
22. **Old Faith vs the Codex:** the Veiled Mother's cunning-folk against the inquisitors, town by town.
23. **Gythwin of the Heath:** Maerwyn's sister-in-craft — where the Old Faith road runs.
24. **The Heath & its barrows, north:** the dark "Moor's Hunger" is only the near edge of.
25. **Stonebay, east:** Ranulf's reach, the Tally-man's master — the buyer of people.
26. **Mossdrowen's failing wood:** Brona's Wardens, undermanned against the Sere.
27. **Count Edmund's March & the Hollowford dig:** Hask's chains — and the Lich below.

## Channel 3 — novelty (honest generator rolls at setup)
28. **[wild: Magical Springs]** On the moor-road north, a spring runs warm and wrong — the Heath's sickness, or its old medicine.
29. **[community: Corrupt Laws]** Justice in the Reach is for sale; the condemned list has prices, if you know whom to pay.
30. **[court: Rival claim]** A rival claimant to Brackenford surfaces — someone disputes Cuthbert's seat, and the strangers are leverage.
31. **[threat: Marauder Force]** The Red Captain's band wasn't all of it — his kin are on the road, and they want their captain back.
32. **[ruin: Rigid Hierarchy]** The undercroft's dead keep rank — an Aureothan order that never got the command to stand down.
33. **[npc: Burnt Out]** Someone here is running on fumes — push them and they break or bolt.
34. **[npc: Great Disappointment]** Someone carries a private failure that drives them — a lever, or a kinship.
35. **[npc quirk: keeps to the shade]** A watcher in the pen-yard who stays out of the light, marking faces — the Tally-man's eyes, or worse.
