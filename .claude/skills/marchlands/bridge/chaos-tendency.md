# Chaos Tendency — Marchlands   (hook: chaos)
- start: 5
- volatility: normal        # leans high — a living world that rarely goes quiet
- floor: deep Heath / the Harrowmark >= 6   # the waking-barrow frontier stays volatile
- flavor: standard          # full chaos; outcomes deliberate, never capricious

Rationale: clocks tick and factions act on their own, so Chaos should climb readily and seldom
drop low — but keep it deliberate. Permadeath is real here, not capricious: roll to find out,
never roll to kill.
